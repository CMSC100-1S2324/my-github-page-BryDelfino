    Hello! I am Bryan Kyle V. Delfino, but you can call me Bry.
    I am originally from Sta. Rosa but moved to Los Baños to pursue
    on my bachelor's degree on Computer Science. I was born on June
    26, 2003. In my childhood, I was not really exposed to programming.
    I actually dream of becoming a mechanical engineer but then reality
    struck me and I thought to myself that maybe I am not for the
    engineering program. I love to play video games, watching TV shows,
    make music and drawing. I love being carefree and just being myself.

    My first exposure in programming was in grade school. This was when
    I was introduced to HTML and CSS. My first exposure of a real
    programming language did not come until I was in Senior High School
    where I took a programming elective which focuses on Java Programming
    (without the OOP concepts). I got offered to study in University of the
    Philippines Los Baños where the degree program offered was BS Computer
    Science. Because of this, I got exposed to other programming languages
    like Python and C. My knowledge on previously learned programming
    concepts are improved and I developed an interest in programming.
    Currently, I am in my third year in the university and so far,
    everything seems to be fine and I am still surviving. As of today, I am
    currently taking three major subjects where each of them focuses on
    Operating Systems, Artificial Intelligence, and Web Programming. I dream
    of working in the cybersecurity industry and someday, I hope to achieve
    that dream.
